Steve Irwin was the son of Bob Irwin , a herpetologist (reptile expert), and Lyn Irwin, who died in 2000 in a car accident. Steve had two more sisters: Joy and Mandy, of which Joy is the eldest. Irwin became known for his spectacular and enthusiastically presented television program " The Crocodile Hunter " (translation: the crocodile hunter ). Irwin owned Australia Zoo in Beerwah, originally a reptile park , founded in 1970 by his father Bob and taken over by Steve in 1992. He ran this park with his wife Terri and his friend Wes Mannion, who had come to work at the park at the age of 14.
